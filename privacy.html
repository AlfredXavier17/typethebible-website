Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or sell any of your personal information. We use Google Analytics to understand general usage patterns, and Formspree to handle contact form submissions. That's it.

Introduction

Welcome to TypeTheBible ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our typing practice website at typethebible.xyz.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information directly. Your typing practice, scores, and progress are stored locally in your browser and never leave your device.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand general usage patterns (such as page views, session duration, and approximate geographic location). This helps us improve the website. Google Analytics may use cookies to collect this data. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Formspree: When you submit a message through our contact form, your email address and message content are processed by Formspree, a third-party form handling service. We only use this information to respond to your inquiry. For more information, see Formspree's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

The limited information collected through third-party services is used solely to:

  • Understand how visitors use our website to improve user experience
  • Respond to messages and feedback submitted through our contact form
  • Monitor website performance and fix technical issues

Local Storage

Your typing progress, statistics, and preferences are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

Cookies

We do not set any first-party cookies. Google Analytics may set cookies to track website usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings or opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties. The only data sharing that occurs is through the third-party services mentioned above (Google Analytics and Formspree), which is necessary for their functionality.

Children's Privacy

Our website is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your personal data, there's nothing for us to delete or modify. However, you can:

  • Clear your local typing data through your browser's localStorage settings
  • Disable cookies and tracking through your browser settings
  • Opt out of Google Analytics using their browser add-on
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the contact form on our home page.
